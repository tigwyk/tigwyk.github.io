Launching the Banano Pay-Test

2021-05-02 :: Tigwyk
#banano  #crypto  #paytest  #payment 

When I forked the accept-nano libraries I noticed they had created a very simple example site that leverages unpkg.com to offer a static one-page payment solution. Since I forked the rest of the library I decided I might as well host the example site somewhere so we could show off the speed of banano, and this little idea was born!

Behold, paytest.banano.live! A one-stop shop for nothing! You can buy absolutely nothing for the low, low price of 0.01 banano. It’s quick, it’s feeless, it’s practically free!

Enter the desired amount of banano you’d like to donate to the cause, click on “Start” and you’ll receive a very simple payment prompt including a QR code. Scan the QR code (or copy and paste the address and amount), send, and you’ll see how quickly the site accepts your payment.

N.B. You are spending real banano during this transaction, it will be used for hosting fees, development, and tips on the lovely Banano discord.
